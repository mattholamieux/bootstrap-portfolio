About

Matthew is an educator, artist, and activist with over ten years of teaching experience in the U.S. and abroad. As an undergraduate at Lewis and Clark College in Portland, OR, he studied foreign languages, with a focus on Russian language and literature. His teaching career began in 2008 when he was a recipient of a Fulbright English Teaching Assistantship in Novosibirsk, Russia. From 2010-2017, he taught in a variety of educational programs for immigrant and refugee students in California, Georgia, and Maine. From 2017-2018, Matthew worked as an English Language Fellow with the U.S. Department of State in Nepal, where he taught university courses on teaching methodology and conducted teacher training workshops for English teachers.
